Tetris

What is Tetris?

Everyone loves Tetris! It's a classic videogame that has survived the test of time. It was developed in 1984 in the USSR for the Electronika 60. The game involves trying to slot together 2D geometric pieces called "tetrominoes" without gaps. Since then the game has been released on a variety of consoles and has undergone many tweaks and variations.

Meet the Tetrominoes

  1. Meet the much loved straight tetrominoe which makes it easy to score a full line!

  2. The classic square tetrominoe can often slot right into place saving the day.

  3. The T tetrominoe often comes in and T-poses on you.

  4. The L tetrominoe often comes with a hard landing and can pose a challenge to fill the row.

  5. The infamous skew tetrominoe often comes in to mess up your game!
